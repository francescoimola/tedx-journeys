Welcome to TEDx University of Greenwich, hosted this year at Bathway Theatre in Woolwich, London.
The theme for this TEDx is Journeys: we'll be joined by nine guests, each discussing the journey of a single individual as a metaphor for the journey of humanity and as an answer to the question of life.
